Affordable Family Law - Pro Bono Help, Unbundled Services

Finding affordable legal help for family law matters can be challenging, especially when facing difficult situations like divorce, child custody disputes, or child support issues. America Family Law Center is committed to providing accessible and affordable family law services to those who need them most.

Understanding Affordable Family Law Options

There are several ways to access affordable family law services:

  • Pro Bono Services: Free legal services provided by attorneys who volunteer their time
  • Unbundled Legal Services: Also known as limited scope representation, where you hire an attorney for specific tasks only
  • Legal Aid Organizations: Nonprofit organizations that provide free or low-cost legal help
  • Assisted Pro Se Services: Help for self-represented litigants to navigate the legal system
  • Sliding Scale Fees: Legal services with fees based on your income

What Are Unbundled Legal Services?

Unbundled legal services, also called limited scope representation, allow you to hire an attorney to handle only specific parts of your case. This can include:

  • Legal advice and consultation
  • Document preparation and review
  • Representation at a specific hearing
  • Negotiation assistance
  • Legal research

This approach allows you to get professional legal help where you need it most while handling other aspects of your case yourself, significantly reducing costs.

The Justice Gap

Many people fall into what is known as the "Justice Gap" - they earn too much to qualify for free legal aid but cannot afford traditional attorney fees. America Family Law Center's Assisted Pro Se services are designed specifically to help people in this situation.

How America Family Law Center Provides Affordable Help

America Family Law Center offers Assisted Pro Se services that provide guidance and support for individuals representing themselves in family law matters. Our services include:

  • Help understanding your legal rights and options
  • Assistance with legal document preparation
  • Guidance on court procedures
  • Information about local resources
  • Support throughout the legal process

Finding Pro Bono Legal Help

Pro bono services are free legal services provided by attorneys. To find pro bono help:

  • Contact your local bar association's lawyer referral service
  • Reach out to legal aid organizations in your area
  • Check with law school clinics
  • Contact America Family Law Center for guidance

Contact Us for Affordable Family Law Help

If you need affordable family law assistance, America Family Law Center is here to help. Contact us today to learn more about our services and how we can assist you with your family law matter.
